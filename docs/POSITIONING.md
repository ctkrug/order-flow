# Positioning

## Audience and benefit

Depthwalk is for finance and computer-science students learning market
microstructure who cannot turn a static explanation of slippage into a useful
mental model. It lets them drag a market order through historical book depth and
see every fill, average-price change, and dollar of slippage form together.

## Product name and tagline

**Display name:** Depthwalk

**Tagline:** Watch market orders turn depth into slippage

## Copy voice

Write like a patient lab instructor: direct, exact, and curious. Lead with what
the visitor can observe, name the real data and Rust matching engine when they
matter, and avoid claims that the interaction itself cannot prove.
